Getting Started

The reason this was created was to give people the truth about what they're eating and hopefully be the easiest way to know how to diet towards your preference whether thats for overall health, cutting, or bulking for strength. This page is inteded for both people trying to eat healthier, get stronger, or look better.

The best way to get good at learning things like this is to stay consistent and taking time out of your day to read more information every day. It helps to take notes as well.

Diet Plans

You might've noticed the plans tab on the navigation bar above, there are multiple plans based on your goal you choose. These plans were created to make it as easy as possible for you to achieve your diet goal. So hopefully less time buying healthier foods, couinting calories, and etc.

Community

The community tab is for others who are trying to connect with others who are trying to learn about nutrition and are on their own journey. Our community is linked through an app called Discord, any questions/concerns or just conversation can be discussed there.

Core Values

Important things to keep in mind while using our program.

  • Consistency
  • Discipline
  • Tenacity
  • Perseverance

If you keep these things you need in mind as you read this material, it will help you reach your goal. It's important to emphasize how much you want it, success can't purely run on motivation; However, with discipline success will come easy.
