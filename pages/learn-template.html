Getting Started

The reason this was created was to give people the truth about what they're eating and hopefully be the easiest way to know how to diet towards your preference whether thats for overall health, cutting, or bulking for strength. This page is inteded for both people trying to eat healthier, get stronger, or look better.

The best way to get good at learning things like this is to stay consistent and taking time out of your day to read more information every day.

Diet Plans

You might've noticed the plans tab on the navigation bar above, the plans tab has multiple plans based on your goal you choose. The plans were created to make it as easy as possible for you to achieve your diet goal. So hopefully less time buying healthier foods, couinting calories, and etc.

Community

The community tab is for others who are trying to connect with others who are trying to learn about nutrition and are on their own journey. Our community is linked through an app called Discord, any questions/concerns or just conversation can be discussed there.

We also greatly appriecate any feedback given to us, as we're always trying to improve the website for people's convience. It is highly recommended you join our community as it will help in case of asking weightlifting or nutrition related questions.
